Audio Processing Limitations & Realistic Scope - Phase 0.9

Far-Field Audio Processing Challenges

Technical Complexity

  • Beamforming Algorithms: Require advanced DSP knowledge and real-time optimization
  • 4-Microphone Array: No ready-made ESP32-S3 libraries available
  • Calibration Requirements: Need acoustic testing and microphone positioning optimization
  • Real-Time Processing: ESP32-S3 computational limits for complex audio algorithms
  • Integration Complexity: Custom algorithms must integrate with ESP-SR wake word detection

Current State of Technology

  • ESP-SR Support: Only single microphone, no array support
  • Open Source: No free 4-mic beamforming implementations for ESP32-S3
  • Commercial Solutions: Expensive licensing ($5,000-$15,000) for professional algorithms
  • Research Level: Most implementations are academic/proprietary
